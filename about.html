About Liyanage Agro Center

Liyanage Agro Center is your trusted partner for all agricultural needs in Sri Lanka. We specialize in high-quality seeds, fertilizers, tools, and expert advice to support both large-scale farmers and home gardeners. With years of experience and dedication, we aim to promote sustainable agriculture through reliable products and outstanding service.

Our Mission

At Liyanage Agro Center, our mission is to empower farmers and gardeners with the best agricultural products and knowledge. We are committed to fostering sustainable farming practices that benefit both the environment and the community.

Customer Satisfaction

Our History

Established in 2015, Liyanage Agro Center began as a small family-owned business with a passion for agriculture. Over the years, we have grown into a trusted name in the industry, serving thousands of satisfied customers across Sri Lanka. Our dedication to quality and innovation has been the cornerstone of our success.
